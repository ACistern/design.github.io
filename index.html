Interior Design

The spaces we live in have a profound effect on us. From colors and materials to proportions, patterns and light, interior design determines the ways we engage with space.

Graphic Design

Graphic Design is a ubiquitous--influencing a broad spectrum of 2D media, including editorial and advertising layouts, websites and cell phone apps, signs, logos, greeting cards and more. Graphic designers choose ways to present information for maximum impact.

Fashion

Fashion brings design to our bodies. Combining fabrics and cuts, fashion designers produce unique ways to frame the human form.
